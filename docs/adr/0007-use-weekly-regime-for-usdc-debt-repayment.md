# Use Weekly Regime For USDC Debt Repayment

USDC debt may be carried while the 1w SOL Supertrend is bearish, but only as defensive carried USDC debt protected by an aggressive ETH hedge or full-short posture. Once the 1w SOL Supertrend turns green, USDC debt repayment becomes a priority before surplus reinvestment or new USDC releverage, because green higher-regime conditions should reduce dollar debt before adding SOL exposure.

## Consequences

The strategy can keep USDC debt through bearish regimes instead of panic-repaying at poor prices, but that debt must be visibly paired with hedge protection. Bullish higher-regime recovery becomes a debt-cleanup phase before the strategy resumes compounding into additional SOL.
